WELCOME
Foxy Loxy Cafe is equal parts coffee shop, bakery and Tex-Mexycantina. Stop in to satiate those caffeine cravings, fill up on our hand-crafted tacos or unwind with a glass of wine. With indoor and outdoor seating you’re bound to find just the right spot so come on and nestle in. Feelin’ a little artsy? We also host artist receptions every first Friday of the month and fire pits in the courtyard every Saturday, where you can enjoy live music by local and regional musicians!

FOXY IS...

Foxy Loxy Cafe boasts a wide range of activities and events. Never a dull moment here!

Tex-Mex

We bring Texas to you! What's a kolache? What does a taco taste like with your espresso? These mysteries and more solved right here at Foxy Loxy Cafe!

Bakery

Our bakers get up bright and early to bring you fresh baked goods seven days a week. We dare you to check out our pastry case and walk away empty handed!

Coffee

We proudly serve and sell locally roasted coffee from The Coffee FoxyRoasting Co. We takes great care and consideration in sourcing our coffees, making sure every cup is a great one.

Events

We are more than just a cup of tasty, locally roasted coffee! We feature talented artists in our gallery, rotating musical acts and fire pits on Saturdays, just to name a few
